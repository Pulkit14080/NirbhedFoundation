Welcome To Our Foundation

Our Foundation Has Over 1500 students

NIRBHED FOUNDATION is an all India- jurisdiction Non Govt. Organisation (registered under Income Tax sec 12A and 80G ) Nirbhed Foundation impart elementary education to Children who are economically weak and are working, school dropouts and children who never went to school. We educate them, involve them in extracurricular activities, give moral education and ethics to these children, and take care of their health. Nirbhed operates day, evening and night classes for such children.

Education

Education is the root of development and success, it is the only tool that never diminish. Child without education, is like a bird without wings.We are here to make the bird fly.

Nourishment

We provide healthy food to keep their mind and body healthy.

Healthcare

We organise regular medical checkup camps and provide free medical facility to our children and their family. Its a 24*7 support.

Awareness

Our major part of work involves spreading awareness about Education, Health, nutrition and Sanitation. We also promote environment protection and ecofriendly activities.

Our Mission

Quality Learning Process

We believe that “if we make children capable and enable them with knowledge and skills, it would help them far more than providing them with materialistic resources.” With this belief we not only impart elementary education but also enables behavioural and moral learning to them. We ensure interactive learning.

Fight Hunger

There is no humiliation more abusive than hunger.Nirbhed has distributed over 72000+ meals till now and number is still increasing. We provide daily meal to our students after study hours. We prepare, collect left over food and also accept food donations. If you want to celebrate any occassion with our children and feed them we welcome you with open heart.

Heath, Hygiene and Sanitation

Nirbhed Foundation put special emphasis on Health, Safety and Sanitation for our kids. We arrange regular medical camps and extend free medical facility to our kids and their Family. We keep proper check over their hygiene conditions and ensure cleaniness.

We Do A Lot To Make All The Children Happy

Audio-visual classes, extra-curricular activities, Games, Art and Craft, Dance classes,Yoga Classes nad lot more
